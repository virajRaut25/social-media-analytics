Analysing Social Media Reaction on Political Issues
using Machine Learning
About
Currently, Twitter is one of the most popular social media platforms that allows its users to express their thoughts on any topic, usually in the form of short messages limited by word count. With a large number of users, Twitter has become a valuable source for analyzing people's behavior and reactions to political issues. However, analyzing the text-based posts can be challenging due to the high dimensionality of the data, making it difficult for models to learn. To overcome this, it is necessary to find an appropriate method to cluster and classify Twitter posts for acceptable results. This study presents the clustering of Twitter users based on the most commonly used words in their reactions to a trending political issue and classifies the tweets as left-wing or right-wing. The results of this study are represented through various visualizations such as dendrograms, histograms, word clouds, bar graphs, etc.
